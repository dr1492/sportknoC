Last updated: April 2026

Overview

SportKnow ("we," "us," or "our") operates sportknow.net. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website. By using SportKnow, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information we collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate and improve SportKnow:

  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, general location (country/city level), device type, and browser type. This is collected automatically via Google Analytics.
  • Search queries — the sports questions you type into SportKnow. These are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI to generate responses and are not stored long-term by us.
  • Cookies — we use cookies for analytics purposes only (Google Analytics). We do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies beyond standard analytics.

We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

How we use your information

  • To provide and improve the SportKnow service
  • To understand how users interact with the site
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To analyze traffic patterns and improve performance

Third party services

SportKnow uses the following third party services that may collect data independently:

  • Google Analytics — collects anonymized usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — may serve personalized ads based on your browsing. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude AI — processes your search queries to generate sports stat responses. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare — provides security and performance services. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

We use cookies to support Google Analytics functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality. By continuing to use SportKnow, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

Your rights (GDPR / UK users)

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Since we collect minimal personal data, most requests can be fulfilled by adjusting your browser's cookie settings. For any data requests, contact us at chuckiecheezitz@gmail.com.

Children's privacy

SportKnow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
